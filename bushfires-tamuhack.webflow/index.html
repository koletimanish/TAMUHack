What is Operation Consolidation?

We're working to make it easy to be prepared for the worst. We want to consolidate data pertinent to emergency situations in one place for the entire world.

Regions Most Affected

Recent history has seen an incredibly rise in cataclysmic events, especially wildfires which decimate everything in their path. What We Consider

Fires
The smoke produced by flames obscures visible imagery, making it difficult to track fires. Fortunately, other wavelengths of light can travel through the haze and provide a clear picture. We use data from the Visible Infrared Imaging Radiometer Suite (VIIRS) to monitor the real-time spread of fires across the world.
Populated Cities
We closely monitor the spread of flames to identify potential dangers to major population centers. Early warnings are critical.
Veterinarians
When humans are in danger, so are their pets. People should be aware of the resources available to help all of their loved ones, not just the human ones.  
Emergency Services
We help people identify the nearest emergency services so that they can get help as soon as possible.  
Animal Refuges
When someone comes across a wild animal in distress, theyshould have the resources to help it or get it in the right hands.
